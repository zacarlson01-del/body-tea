Escrow Chapter Oversight and Professional Standards

The Escrow Chapter operates as a structured oversight body dedicated to maintaining professional accountability, ethical compliance, and operational integrity within the massage therapy profession. Established to respond to growing concerns around practitioner verification, liability exposure, and trust between providers and clients, the chapter has evolved into a nationally respected framework for standards enforcement and professional assurance.

Originally developed to address challenges associated with malpractice coverage and practitioner credibility, the program expanded beyond its initial regional scope to serve professionals across the United States. Today, it functions as a reliable and cost-effective model for safeguarding professional practice while supporting long-term industry stability.

The chapter’s standards framework is supported by Allied Professionals Insurance Company, a Risk Retention Group, Inc., a recognized leader in liability coverage for alternative health care professionals. Allied maintains strong financial stability ratings, including A Prime Unsurpassed from Demotech and A Minus Excellent from AM Best.
